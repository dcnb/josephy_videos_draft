--- title: About Idaho Queered layout: default ---

Bennett is collaborating with the Center for Digital Inquiry and Learning (CDIL) to collect, digitize and archive oral histories from members of the LGBTQ community across Idaho. These oral histories will be made publicly available via the Idaho Queered website over the course of the next two years.

Idaho Queered is supported by the National Endowment for the Humanities, the Pride Foundation, the Idaho Women's Giving Circle, and the Latah County Community Foundation.

“We want to get a contemporary and diverse history represented. The goal is to document histories in every county of Idaho. It’s history. I want to document history. It’s just getting the word out and convincing people their life is interesting enough to tell.”

Denise Bennett
